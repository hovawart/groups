About Our Group

Building a caring and supportive community.

Our Mission

Our volunteer support group exists to provide a welcoming environment where people can talk one-on-one with volunteer support, share experiences, receive encouragement, and connect with others who understand the challenges of mental health.

Our Values

  • Respect for every individual.
  • Compassion and understanding.
  • Confidentiality within the group.
  • Support without judgment.
