About Our Group

Building a caring and supportive community.

Our Mission

Our volunteer support group exists to provide a welcoming environment where people can talk one-on-one with volunteer support, share experiences, receive encouragement, and connect with others who understand the challenges of mental health.

Our Values

  • Respect for every individual.
  • Compassion and understanding.
  • Confidentiality within the group.
  • Support without judgment.

What We're Not

We are not licensed mental health professionals, counselors, therapists, or psychologists, and we do not provide clinical mental health treatment or diagnosis. Our team is made up of compassionate volunteers who offer peer support, a listening ear, and a safe space to talk. Many of our volunteers have personal experience with mental health challenges or have supported loved ones through difficult times.

Our conversations are not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or need clinical support, we encourage you to seek help from a qualified professional.
