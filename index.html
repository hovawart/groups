Harbor Idaho - Volunteer Support

A welcoming community offering peer support and encouragement from volunteers. You're not alone.

Who We Are

We are a volunteer-led mental health support group dedicated to providing a safe, welcoming place where people can connect, share experiences, and encourage one another. Our goal is to help build a supportive community where everyone feels heard and respected. Volunteers meet with you one-on-one over the phone or chat. Email us at email@example.com to connect with a volunteer.

What We Offer

We offer regular support meetings, provide a friendly environment for conversation, and help connect people with community resources when additional support is needed.
